# Delta for Landing

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Focused Product Presentation
The landing page MUST feature a focused phase-1 product set while acknowledging the broader line.

#### Scenario: Hero products
- WHEN the products section is rendered
- THEN the page SHALL prominently feature Pets Basics, Pets Calm, Pets Care & Comfort, and Pets Ahiflower Oil

#### Scenario: Extended line
- WHEN the broader catalog is shown
- THEN it SHALL appear in a secondary line-expansion section rather than competing with the hero products

### Requirement: Professional Entry Point
The landing page MUST include a visible but non-dominant path for veterinarians and collaborators.

#### Scenario: Professional CTA
- WHEN a professional visitor scans the page
- THEN they SHALL find a dedicated resources or contact pathway without disrupting the B2C narrative
