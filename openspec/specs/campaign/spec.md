# Campaign Specification

## Purpose

Define the strategic marketing foundation for LifePlus Pets Spain across B2C, B2B, creative direction, and channel priorities.

## Requirements

### Requirement: Dual-Audience Strategy
The campaign MUST address both dog owners and professional stakeholders.

#### Scenario: Consumer messaging
- WHEN content is created for B2C channels
- THEN it SHALL emphasize daily wellness, simplicity, visible care, and premium trust

#### Scenario: Professional messaging
- WHEN content is created for B2B channels
- THEN it SHALL emphasize clear formulations, responsible messaging, and practical materials for clinics or collaborators

### Requirement: Channel Roles
Each primary channel MUST have a defined job in the campaign.

#### Scenario: Instagram role
- WHEN Instagram content is planned
- THEN it SHALL function as the main discovery, inspiration, and visual desire channel

#### Scenario: LinkedIn role
- WHEN LinkedIn content is planned
- THEN it SHALL function as the main professional credibility and outreach channel

#### Scenario: SEO role
- WHEN SEO content is planned
- THEN it SHALL function as the main demand-capture and authority channel in Spanish

### Requirement: Established Visual Continuity
The campaign MUST remain visually consistent with the Ahiflower asset series already present in the workspace.

#### Scenario: New creative production
- WHEN new campaign assets are produced
- THEN they SHALL retain the brand palette, dog-lifestyle imagery, premium product visibility, and strong headline hierarchy used in the Ahiflower materials

### Requirement: Launch Sequencing
The project MUST finish missing Ahiflower continuity assets before broadening into unrelated concept sets.

#### Scenario: Existing creative gaps
- WHEN the current asset inventory is reviewed
- THEN missing Ahiflower pieces SHALL be treated as active priority work
