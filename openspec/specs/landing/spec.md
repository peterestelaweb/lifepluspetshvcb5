# Landing Specification

## Purpose

Define the required structure and behavior of the LifePlus Pets Spain landing page.

## Requirements

### Requirement: Clear Brand Introduction
The landing page MUST communicate what LifePlus Pets is within the first screenful.

#### Scenario: Hero section
- WHEN a visitor lands on the page
- THEN the page SHALL present a premium dog-wellness positioning, a concise value proposition, and a primary call to action

### Requirement: Focused Product Presentation
The landing page MUST feature a focused phase-1 product set while acknowledging the broader line.

#### Scenario: Hero products
- WHEN the products section is rendered
- THEN the page SHALL prominently feature Pets Basics, Pets Calm, Pets Care & Comfort, and Pets Ahiflower Oil

#### Scenario: Extended line
- WHEN the broader catalog is shown
- THEN it SHALL appear in a secondary line-expansion section rather than competing with the hero products

### Requirement: Ahiflower Priority Story
The landing page MUST include a dedicated Ahiflower Oil section because it has the strongest creative support in the workspace.

#### Scenario: Ahiflower detail block
- WHEN the Ahiflower section is presented
- THEN it SHALL explain skin and coat support, botanical omega value, and routine ease without medical claims

### Requirement: Professional Entry Point
The landing page MUST include a visible but non-dominant path for veterinarians and collaborators.

#### Scenario: Professional CTA
- WHEN a professional visitor scans the page
- THEN they SHALL find a dedicated resources or contact pathway without disrupting the B2C narrative

### Requirement: Routine Simplicity
The landing page MUST reduce perceived complexity of use.

#### Scenario: Routine explanation
- WHEN the usage section is shown
- THEN the page SHALL present simple, low-friction routine guidance rather than dense technical detail
